THE EVENT The SIGINT is an annual three-day conference on technical and social aspects of our digital society. It offers lectures and workshops on a multitude of topics. This year, it will take place from July 5th to 7th at KOMED im Mediapark in Cologne, Germany.

The SIGINT has always been organized by the community and appreciates all kinds of participation. You are encouraged to contribute by volunteering to this event.

Please take a look at our FAQ if you have any questions about SIGINT. Updated information are covered by the CCC Events Blog or via Twitter (@sigint).

You can contribute to this website by forking our GitHub repository and sending us a pull request.
