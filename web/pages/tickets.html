TICKETS Our presale is now over, but you can still buy all tickets at the venue.

Category Price Comment
Standard € 60 no separate invoice
Supporter € 90 no separate invoice, but good feeling included
Business € 250 comes with an invoice that includes name and address

Day ticket € 30 sold only at the venue

Children under 12 in attendance of an adult get in for free. A single person officially accompanying a handicapped person gets in for free.

Please note that you will only be able to pay in cash at the venue. We do not take credit cards or debit cards of any kind. There are plenty ATMs around Mediapark, so this should not be an issue.

If you or someone you know cannot afford to pay the full price, send the organizers an e-mail. Please tell us a bit about you or him/her, why you or this person can't pay the full price and why he or she should take part in SIGINT nevertheless. Usually we agree on something.

If you have further questions about our tickets, check the FAQ or contact us.
