THE EVENT The SIGINT is an annual three-day conference on technical and social aspects of our digital society. It offers lectures and workshops on a multitude of topics.

The SIGINT has always been organized by the community and appreciates all kinds of participation. You are encouraged to contribute by volunteering to this event.

Please take a look at our FAQ if you have any questions about SIGINT. Updated information are covered by the CCC Events Blog or via Twitter (@sigint).

You can contribute to this website by forking its GitHub repository and sending us a pull request.
